What We Manufacture

Solid surface, shaped for every space.

From raw sheet stock to fully fabricated fixtures, every piece leaves our Mumbai facility inspected for colour consistency, thickness and finish.

Corian Solid Surface Sheets

Our bestselling line — non-porous acrylic solid surface sheets in a wide range of colours and thicknesses, cut to order.

Kitchen Countertops

Seamlessly joined worktops with integrated sinks and backsplashes, fabricated to millimetre-accurate site dimensions.

Wash Basins & Vanities

Moulded and thermoformed basin counters for homes, hotels and hospitals — hygienic, jointless, easy to clean.

Wall Cladding & Panels

Large-format acrylic panels for feature walls, hospital interiors and commercial facades with a flawless finish.

Reception & Office Counters

Custom Fabrication

Bespoke shapes, inlays and colour-matched runs manufactured to architect and interior designer specifications.
